& Department of Mechanical Engineering

Supported by

About ICACT-18


    The International Conference on Automation and Computing Technologies (ICACT-18) is aimed to promote the scientific platform for exchange of ideas between the researchers and promoting innovative aspects in Electrical, Electronics and Mechanical Engineering. The panel discussion encounters the existing challenges and solution suggested by experts which helps to fulfill the department in computing and automation. Techno era expects fast pace in all walks of life, i.e. automation, entertainment, health care, database, e-egoverence and in various subjects. ICACT–18 will be held at the Velammal Institute of Technology on 14th March,2018. This conference is an initiation to build a technical society for future collaboration and specific goal of globalization to achieve international standards.


CALL FOR PAPERS:

Research papers based on original work is invited for presentation in the conference. Extended Abstracts can be send by email to icact18@velammalitech.edu.in
      Note: All the papers submitted will be reviewed and published in the Conference Proceedings. Further arrangements will be made to publish the selected papers in the peer reviewed journals.

CONFERENCE TOPICS:

With an emphasis on reproducibility and appreciation of theoretical work, the topic areas covered by ICACT-18 include, but are not limited to:

Electronics and Communication Engineering

  • Ad-hoc and Sensor Networks
  • Biomedical Signal Processing and Analysis, Computer-Aided Diagnosis
  • Cognitive Radio
  • Image, Audio, Video Processing, Analysis and Applications
  • MEMS
  • Mobile/Wireless networks
  • Network Security
  • Optical Networks
  • Satellite Communications
  • Smart Antennas
  • Statistical Signal Processing and Modeling

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

  • Distributed Generation, Fuel Cells and Renewable Energy Systems
  • Electrical Machinery
  • Intelligent Control Systems
  • Measuring Technology and Instruments
  • Nuclear Energy
  • Power Quality
  • Power Electronics and Drives
  • Power System and its Automation

Mechanical Engineering

  • Industrial Automation
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
  • Manufacturing
  • Material Science
  • Mechanical Design
  • Automotive Engineering
  • Mechatronics
  • Heat Transfer
  • Thermal Engineering
  • Renewable Energy

Best Paper will be awarded for each session
