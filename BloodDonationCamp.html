Blood Donation Camp

Blood Donation Camp (IRCS-YRC-UNIT), 3rd August, 2015

Velammal Institute of Technology organized Blood Donation Camp on 03.08.2015 at 9:30 A.M , in the college campus.The volunteers involved staff and students who donated blood to the Indian Red Cross Society, Egmore, Chennai. There were approximately 216 members who donated the blood in the camp Prof.K.Razak, Advisor of Velammal Institute of Technology gave the Inaugural address and stressed the importance of donating blood. Dr.T.Chandrashekar, Principal of Velammal Institute of Technology addressed the gathering about the advantages of blood donation and motivated the students to give blood to the needy people.


Dr.M.D.Vinayagam, Chief doctor, Indian Red Cross Society , Egmore, Tamilnadu Branch said about the awareness of Blood Donation camp. He was very much pleased and happy to see the volunteers who has come forward to donate blood. He added that he expects the same in future too. Mr.Namachivayam,PRO, Indian Red Cross Society accompained the camp.The chief guest was felicitated by the staff co-ordinator Mr.P.Deivendran (IT-Dept.,). The camp was successfully organized by Mr.M.Selvavinayagam, YRC Student co-ordinator.



 

 

Blood Donation Camp @Velammal I-Tech, Organized by Youth Red Cross Society
