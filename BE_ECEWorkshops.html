One Day Workshop On "WAVELET TRANSFORM AND IT’S APPLICATIONS" 09th March 2015.

One day workshop on WAVELET TRANSFORM AND ITS APPLICATIONS was organised in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering on 09th March 2015. Mr. S. Ilaiyaraja HOD-ECE welcomed the participants and the guests. Dr. T. Chandrasekhar, Principal felicitated the guests and delivered inaugural address. Dr. G.Thirugnanam, Annamalai University gave a presentation about Digital Image Watermarking- A data hiding technique and Dr.T.Mangayarkarasi, Annamalai University gave a presentation about Wavelet Transform and its Application to Images. Hands on training were given to the participants for the same.
