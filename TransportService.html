Transport Service

TRANSPORT SERVICE: The Institution aim is to provide safe transport arrangement to students for pick-up from their destinations to the College in time and vice versa in the evening while return for home. Institution has carefully drafted routes to cover all parts in the City and outskirts to ensure that students are satisfied with the routings provided by Instituion. Institution ensure that all buses reach college between 8.00 am and 8.15 am for start up of classes at 8.30 am. It also sees that students reach their destination in time after their depart from college at 4.10 pm.
