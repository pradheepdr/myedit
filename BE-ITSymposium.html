National level Technical Symposium - INSIGNIA’ 16

    National level symposium - INSIGNIA ’16

      

(From left : Ms. M. Suchithra H.O.D, IT, Ms. Kalaiselvi Raman, Associate Director (Insurance) – CTS, Dr. T. Chandrashekar, Principal Velammal I.Tech)

On 16th July, 2016 A 5th National level symposium called INSIGNIA ’16 was organized by Department of Information Technology, Velammal Institute of Technology, Chennai. The event started with a welcome note of MS M Suchithra, HOD-IT with thoughts on motto of the event and its deliverables by welcoming the gathering.

Ms. Kalaiselvi Raman, Associate director (Insurance), CTS, was the chief guest for the event. The presidential address was delivered by Dr. T. Chandrasekhar, Principal, Velammal I Tech with emphasize on the need for acquiring latest technology skill set to match the needs of the industry. The chief guest was felicitated as a token of gratitude by the principal. The chief guest Ms Kalaiselvi Raman shared her thoughts on evolvement of technologies over time and how technology drives the current business processes. The chief guest also spoke about the trending technologies and their essentiality to acquire those skills which has its diverse career opportunities in coming days.

The annual souvenir which comprised the achievements, thinking’s and artistic capabilities was released by the dignitaries, followed by the screening of the event video which contained various technology achievements of the world and a description of events which are part of the symposium. The inauguration ceremony ended with vote of thanks by Ms V Kushmitha, Vice president, INSIGNIA’16 with notes on thankfulness to management, department of IT and all kind hearts who worked hard for the event to come in reality.


National level Technical Symposium - INSIGNIA’ 15

    National level symposium - INSIGNIA ’15

      

(From left : Dr. T. Chandrashekar, Principal Velammal I.Tech, V. Krishnamurthy, Director NIELIT, . Ms. M. Suchitra H.O.D, IT)

Chennai 8th August 15 :‘Insignia 2015’,A National level Technical Symposium was held at Velammal Institute of Technology organized by the Department of Information Technology on 8th August 2015. The opening ceremony was started with a vibrant quote by Miss. Nithya with the gracious presence of the Chief Guest Shri. V. Krishnamurthy, Director-In-charge of NIELIT, Chennai centre and Dr. T. Chandrashekar, Principal Of Velammal ITech. The auspicious occasion begins with lighting the lamp, all the dignitaries on the dais were requested to light the lamp. Ms. M. Suchitra Head of the Department delivered the Welcome Address, followed by Presidential Address, by Dr. T. Chandrashekar, Principal of Velammal ITech.

The guest was felicitated by the Principal as a token of gratitude. Shri. V. Krishnamurthy delivered the Guest Address and lectured on Cyber Security which turned out to be an eye opening session. Insignia 2015 was a great success with the presence of large number of students from various institutions around the country. All the events were conducted in an organized way and the participants were given prize amount and certificates during the valedictory ceremony. The event came to an end with a Vote of Thanks delievered by Mr. S. Subash, Student Cordinator of ‘INSIGNIA 2015’.


National level Technical Symposium – INSIGNIA ‘14

    National level symposium - INSIGNIA ’14

      

Department of Information Technology organised a national level technical symposium “INSIGNIA-14” on 6th September, 2014 at “Velammal Knowledge Park”, Panchetti.

The occasion commenced with lighting of the lamp by the dignitaries on the dais to begin with a divine touch. The inauguration then continued with the welcome address given by Mrs. Selvakanmani, Head of Department (Information Technology) followed by from the presidential address from Prof. K. Razak, advisor, Velammal Institute of technology and Dr. T. Chandrashekar, Principal, Velammal Institute of Technology addressed the gathering.

The guest of honour Mr. Sharan Gurunathan , General Manager, Avnet-services Business solutions delivered a thought provoking speech on what industries demand and made the students aware of the present technology. Next the Souvenir of INSIGNIA-14 was unveiled by the dignitaries on the dais.Last but not the least ,the official video of INSIGNIA-14 was unleased with a thunderous applause.

The inauguration was concluded with the vote of thanks given by Ms. G. Varsha, general secretary of INSIGNIA’14.


National level Technical Symposium – INSIGNIA’13

    National level symposium - INSIGNIA ’13

      

Department of Information Technology organised a National level technical symposium “INSIGNIA-13” on 14th september, 2013 at Velammal Knowledge Park(college premises), Panchetti.

The programme started at 9 AM with an enthusiastic quote by Mas. K. HemathKumar and Ms. Kavya followed by the “invocatory song”. All the auspicious occasions commence with the lighting of the lamp, the guest of honour Mr. Venkat Ramani ,co-founder MELIO SYSTEMS was asked to light the lamp to begin the inauguration with a divine touch.

The inauguration then continued with the welcome address given by Mrs. Suchithra, Head of Department (Information Technology) followed by the presidential address from Dr. T. Chandrashekar, Principal, Velammal Institute of Technology. The guest of honour was felicitated by Dr. T. Chandrashekar, Principal of Velammal Institute of Technology. Next the souvenir of INSIGNIA-13 was unvieled by the dignitaries on the dais. Last but not the least ,the official video of INSIGNIA-13 was unleased with a thunderous applause.

The inauguration was concluded with the vote of thanks given by Mr. Vinayak Ram, student co-ordinator.
