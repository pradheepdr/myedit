Conferences


Our Department organized the Second National Conference on Advance Computing Technology (NaCACT'13) on May 2, 2013. This conference aims to provide a platform to share Academic and Industrial experiences. It also paves a way for converging various technologies to offer solutions to Industrial problems.
A total of about 120 technical papers were received from post graduate students, faculty members and scientists from R & D organizations covering a wide spectrum of areas, viz. Bioinformatics, Network Security, Data Mining & Warehousing, Distributed Database, Grid Computing, Mobile Computing and Wireless Networks, Nano Technology/Computing, Pervasive/Adaptive Computing, Soft Computing, Web and Open Source Technology, Signal and Image Processing, Adhoc and Sensor networks, Cloud computing & its applications and Robotics. These papers were peer reviewed by technical experts and about 60 papers have been selected for presentation. This volume is a record of current research in the field of issues and trends in advanced computing and it is hoped that it will be a trigger for expanding the realms of research.
"National Conference on Advanced Computing Technology ( NaCACT13 ) organized by department of CSE on 02nd May 2013"
