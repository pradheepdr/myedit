Department of Placement & Training:

OBJECTIVE: To Make our engineers and MBAs future ready
What We do? The Department interacts with reputed organizations all over the country and arranges campus placement for final year students. It makes effort to send students for in-plant training, Internship, Summer Project, Soft Skills training, Guest lectures and Industrial visits to develop their technical ability and gain practical knowledge. Technical symposiums and club activities are held regularly to train students to be industry ready.
Why Velammal Institute of Technology :

Our Strengths
Velammalitech offers the highest number of students meeting the eligibility criteria for recruitment, as we have the highest pass percentage in University examinations
Best Placement ratio (. No. of students attended vs. No. of students recruited) in most of the recruiting companies
A strong in house training team supporting students on spoken English and personality development. Our team of i soft skill trainers provide comprehensive training in the areas of placement preparedness, communication skills and personality development, thus empowering our students with knowledge skill and attitude required for a secure future. 
Post placement training to students to make them 'Plug & Play' in industry in the areas of Technical knowledge and Personality traits
Activities
Career Awareness sessions
Model Aptitude tests, Mock Group discussions & Interviews to excel in the Recruitment process
Higher studies counseling
Entrepreneurship Cell
Arranging In-plant training, Industrial visits, Projects, Guest lectures and other Industry-Institute interaction activities
